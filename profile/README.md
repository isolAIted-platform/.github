## What is isolAIted?

isolAIted is a 100% on-premise agentic AI platform designed for professionals and organizations handling sensitive data. It installs directly on local hardware, ensuring complete digital sovereignty and eliminating unpredictable API or subscription costs.

### Core Pillars
* **Sovereignty and Privacy:** Outbound connections are blocked; corporate data never leaves your local network.
* **Continuity and Zero Cost:** Operates 24/7 with no per-query fees or API rate limits.
* **Governance and Control:** Granular permissions, audit trails, and version-controlled agents for full operational control.

## Operational Levels

Deploy a secure workspace in 10 minutes and scale with three levels:
